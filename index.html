Remembering Kobe Bryant

Summary

TimeLine

  • 1996:

    Declared for the NBA Draft and was selected by the Charlotte Hornets with 13th overall pick.

  • 1997:

    Won the Slam Dunk Contest and was named NBA(National Basketball Association) All-Star Player.

  • 2000-2003:

    Won 3 consecutive NBA Championships with the help of his former teammate, Shaquille O'neal.

  • 2004:

    After losing the NBA Finals, his former teammate Shaquille O'neal was traded to a different team, leaving Kobe Bryant as the "cornerstone" of the Los Angeles Lakers.

  • 2006:

    He scored a career-high of 81 points in one game. This game was the second most points scored in a single game in the history of NBA.

  • 2009-2010:

    Led the team to two consecutive championships and was named NBA Finals MVP on both occassions.

  • 2013:

    Suffered a torn Achilles tendon and later on suffered season-ending injuries to his knee and shoulder.

  • 2015-2016:

    Retired after this year's season.

  • 2020:

    On January 26th, 2020, Kobe Bryant, the NBA MVP who had a 20-year career with the Lakers, was killed Sunday when the helicopter he was traveling in crashed and burst into flames in the hills above Calabasas. His daughter Gianna, 13, was also on board and died along with seven others.
    On February 7th, Bryant and his daughter were buried in a private funeral in Pacific View Memorial Park in the Corona del Mar neighborhood of Newport Beach, California.
